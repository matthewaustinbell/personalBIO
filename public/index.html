About Me

Working for my self as a musician was aspirational to say the least, but in under a year of moving to nashville it was a reality. Although it was a just paying the rent and the grocery bill reality, a reality none the less. I wanted to have impact. I remember telling someone, I bet it was great to have been a farmer, but what about the guy who invented the pitch fork? He seemingly changed so many peoples lives by scratching his own itch. One day, finding my self at a music and business conference some years later, I saw people that were creating things using technology that impacted so many. I want make something that helps people. Software is the why because software is the how.
